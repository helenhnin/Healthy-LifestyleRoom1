Objective of our website

We, “Fellow For Your Wellness” are willing to educate and spread awareness about how a healthy living is crucial for us, some misunderstanding beliefs and more knowledge about it which is especially aimed at our precious Myanmar citizens. Since our country is still a developing country, there are many ongoing issues like the increasing rate of obesity , the prevalence of harmful chemical foods, the side-effects of mental illness and the lack of awareness about those which could even lead to death in the long term. As a patriotic citizen, we feel very sorry and indignant about those unpleasant problems around our country and that is why we decided to create this website in order to contribute in protecting against them.

Targeted Audience

Although our targeted audiences are Myanmar citizens, everyone regardless of age, gender, and race can freely explore if they’re interested.There are also many helpful information and knowledge that are also crucial for diverse people such as about the ways to make yourself relax, awareness about overdose which are quite common in other countries if I'm not mistaken. Hence, everyone can feel free to explore our website at anytime and anywhere.
